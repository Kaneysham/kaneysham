Denzels Recipes

Almond Croissants: A delicate pastry filled with the sweet nutty goodness of almond paste, dusted with powdered sugar, and baked to a golden perfection.

Indgredients
  1. 1 Sheet of puff pastry.
  2. 1/2 cup of almond paste.
  3. 1/4 cup of powdered sugar.
  4. 1 Egg (for egg wash).
Instructions
  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
  2. Roll out the puff pastry and cut it into triangles.
  3. Spread a thin layer of almond paste on each triangle.
  4. Roll each triangle from the base to the tip and curve into a crescent shape.
  5. Brush with egg wash and sprinkle with powdered sugar.
  6. And bake for 15-20 minutes until it’s golden brown.

Sour Dough Bread: A community favorite, boasting a crusty exterior and a soft, airy interior, embodying the classic sourdough charm.

Indgredients
  1. 1 cup sourdough starter.
  2. 1 1/2 cups warm water.
  3. 4 cups bread flour
  4. 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
Instructions
  1. Mix the starter, water, and 3 cups of flour in a large bowl to create the sticky dough.
  2. Add salt, and remaining flour 1 tablespoon at a time until they are fully incorporated.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 15 minutes
  4. Once the kneading is done, place the dough in a lightly greased bowl, cover it, and let it rise for about 5 hours.
  5. Shape the dough into a loaf, and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  6. When you are ready to bake, preheat your oven to 450°F and bake for about 30 minutes until it's golden brown.

Classic Carrot Cake: An homage to tradition and familial bonds, enriched with the natural sweetness of carrots and a crunch of walnuts, it holds a special place in his heart as it was his late grandfather's favorite. Every year on his grandfather's birthday, Denzel bakes this cake to honor his memory and the bond they shared over the love of baking.

Indgredients
  1. 2 cups all-purpose flour.
  2. 2 cups granulated sugar.
  3. 1 teaspoon baking soda.
  4. 2 teaspoons cinnamon.
  5. 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  6. 1 cup vegetable oil.
  7. 4 large eggs.
  8. 2 cups grated carrots.
  9. 1 cup crushed pineapple.
  10. 1/2 cup chopped walnuts.
Instructions
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix together the flour, sugar, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Stir in the oil and eggs until smooth.
  4. Fold in the carrots, pineapple, and walnuts and then pour the batter into a greased baking pan and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  5. Allow the cake to cool before frosting with cream cheese frosting.

Denzels Baking Tips

Denzel doesnt only create, he wants to help others during their baking journey also. These are a few baking tips for anyone whos just starting their baking journey.

  1. Measure your indgredients accurately.
  2. Allow your eggs & butter to reach room temperature before baking.
  3. Rotating the baking tray halfway through the baking time for an even bake.
